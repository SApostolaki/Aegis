About Aegis

Aegis is a web application developed to support the exploration of seismic hazard and risk information. The name “Aegis” was chosen to reflect the platform’s role as an informative tool—offering users a clear view of seismic conditions across different locations. In Greek mythology, the Aegis was the shield of Zeus, symbolizing protection and support. Similarly, this application aims to offer accessible insights based on established models and data, without replacing official assessments or serving as a tool for seismic design.
